about us

We don't just Trading, we build your business.

Shabab Ribab Trading Ltd is a pioneering trading company in Bangladesh. We have introduced industrial robots for the first time in the country, helping streamline manufacturing processes and boost productivity. We are the sole distributor and agency of Cell Nature (Thailand) Co., Ltd, and we import high-quality skincare products from Korea and Thailand. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer service has helped us establish a strong reputation in the market. We are dedicated to introducing innovative products and solutions that meet the diverse needs of our customers.
